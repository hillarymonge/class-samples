Top 4 Best Anime

1.Dragonball Z

One of the most known anime in the world, created
by Akira Toriyama, its a must see for all anime lovers.
Starting with dragonball, then dragonballz, following it
dragonball GT, and airing right now dragonball super.

2.Fairy Tail

Magic, dragons, and friends that are like family, best
mix ever, to create an amazing anime, that will make
you laugh and just have a great time.

3.Sword Art Online

If you are a fan of video games, you will love this anime,
all about a video game gone wrong, swords and a love story,
what more do you want!

4.Tokyo Ghoul

In tokyo, ghouls live among them, the story of a boy
who becomes one against his own will, truly intresting, and bloody
gore to, for those who like it.
